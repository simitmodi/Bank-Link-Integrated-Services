Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I reset my password?
Visit the Forgot Password page and follow the instructions.

2. Can I use emojis in my password?
Sadly, no. Our system only accepts letters, numbers, and standard symbols (e.g., !, @, #).

3. Is "Password123" a good password?
No! Use a unique password with 12+ characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

4. Why is my cat walking on my keyboard?
While we can’t explain cat behavior, we recommend securing your workspace during critical tasks. 😺

5. Can I sign up if I’m under 18?
You must be at least 18 years old to create an account.

6. Why is the login button blue?
It’s part of our branding! The blue (#2a477e) represents trust and security.

7. Can I delete my account?
Yes. Go to Account Settings and select "Delete Account." Note: This action is irreversible.

8. Do you store my credit card info?
No. We use secure third-party payment processors and never store full card details.

9. What if I forget my birthdate?
Contact support with a government ID for verification.

10. Is there a dark mode?
Not yet, but we’re considering it! For now, enjoy the calming #faf7ed background.

11. Can I use my gaming chair as collateral?
Unfortunately, no. We only accept traditional collateral types.

12. Why am I getting spam emails?
We don’t send spam! Check if emails are from official @banklink.com addresses.

13. Can I pay with cryptocurrency?
Not yet, but we’re exploring blockchain integration.

14. What happens if I die?
Our Terms of Service outline account inheritance policies.

15. Can I use this on Mars?
Our services are currently Earth-only. Martian internet not supported. 🌍
